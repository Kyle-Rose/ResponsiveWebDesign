Features

Amazon Alexa is a voice-controlled smart assistant that lets you interact with your devices, play music, manage your calendar, set reminders, and get news or weather updates. It integrates with smart home devices to control lights, thermostats, and locks, and supports thousands of “Skills” to extend functionality, like cooking, fitness, or games. Alexa also enables hands-free communication, voice shopping on Amazon, and customizable routines, all while offering privacy controls for your data.

How It Works

Amazon Alexa is a cloud-based voice assistant that listens for a wake word like “Alexa.” Once activated, it records your voice command and sends it securely to Amazon’s cloud servers, where artificial intelligence and natural language processing interpret your request. Alexa then retrieves the relevant information or performs the action — such as playing music, controlling smart home devices, answering questions, or setting reminders — and responds back through your device.
