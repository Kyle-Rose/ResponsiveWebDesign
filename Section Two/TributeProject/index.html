Thomas Edison

Thomas Edison was an American inventor best known for improving the light bulb and developing the phonograph and motion picture camera.

Thomas Edison
Thomas Edison Holding a lightbulb.
  • Edison did not invent the light bulb, but he made it practical for everyday use by improving the filament and design.
  • He invented the phonograph.
  • He created the first industrial research lab.
